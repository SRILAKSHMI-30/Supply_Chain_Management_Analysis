# 🚚 Supply Chain Management Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project provides a detailed **supply chain performance analysis** through an interactive dashboard.  
It consolidates key metrics such as **quantity sold, shipping status, revenue, profit, regional performance, and yearly trends**, enabling stakeholders to monitor efficiency and profitability across the supply chain.

---

## 🔑 Key Features
- **Order & Shipping Metrics**
  - Total Quantity Sold: 1,198  
  - Orders Count: 1,198  
  - Shipping Status: Completed (416), Pending (391), Cancelled (391)  

- **Financial Performance**
  - Revenue: ₹60,32,814.18  
  - Sales Price: ₹6,00,553.22  
  - Profit: ₹26,00,285.64  

- **Regional Profit Distribution**
  - East: ₹7,02,405.13  
  - North: ₹6,75,958.54  
  - South: ₹6,16,764.83  
  - West: ₹6,05,157.14  

- **Category & Region Analysis**
  - Quantity sold across categories (A, B, C, D) segmented by East, North, South, West.

- **Yearly Trends**
  - Revenue growth from 2023–2026  
  - Quantity sold over years (peak in 2024, decline in 2026)

- **State-Level Insights**
  - Top 5 Sales States: Pennsylvania, Delaware, Texas, Wisconsin, Louisiana  
  - Top 5 Ratings States: Pennsylvania, California, Michigan, Delaware, Wisconsin  

---

## 📈 Insights
- **Peak Year:** 2024 had the highest revenue (₹20,46,338.38) and quantity sold (4,269).  
- **Profit Leaders:** East region generated the highest profit.  
- **State Highlight:** Pennsylvania leads both in sales and ratings.  
- **Shipping Challenge:** ~33% of orders were cancelled, indicating potential inefficiencies in logistics.  
- **Trend:** Decline in 2026 suggests need for operational review.
